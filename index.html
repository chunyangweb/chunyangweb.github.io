Story

A thread across borders.

I did not come to finance just to read numbers.
I came to understand the stories behind them.

From China to the UK, then France, my path has moved across markets, cultures, and languages. Finance has been the thread — from financial markets to operational finance, from analysis to strategy — but curiosity has always been the force moving me forward.

I learned French from zero to a professional working level in less than two years.
That journey taught me more than a language; it taught me how fast I can rebuild, adapt, and grow when nothing is yet familiar.

Today, I am learning a new language: the one between finance and technology.
A language of data, automation, and AI — turning analysis into insight, and insight into smarter decisions.

Dynamic, resilient, and curious, I am not just building a finance career.
I am building the next version of it.
